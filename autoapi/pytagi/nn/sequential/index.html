get_norm_mean_var() → dict[source]#

Get the mean and variance of the normalization layer. :returns: A dictionary containing the mean and variance of the normalization layer.

each key is the layer name and the value is a tuple of 4 arrays: mu_batch: mean of the batch var_batch: variance of the batch mu_ema_batch: mean of the exponential moving average (ema) of the batch var_ema_batch: variance of the ema of the batch

get_lstm_states() → dict[source]#

Get the LSTM states for all LSTM layers as a dictionary.

Returns:

A dictionary where each key is the layer index (int) and each value is a 4-tuple

dict
